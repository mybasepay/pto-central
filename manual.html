A. What is PTO Central

Everyone

PTO Central is myBasePay's company tool for managing paid time off in one place. Use it to submit PTO requests, cancel PTO, track your requests and their statuses, and give the company clear PTO visibility on the appropriate calendars. Managers use it to review and decide requests, and HR uses it to oversee all PTO activity.

B. Submit a PTO Request

EveryoneHR / Admin for on-behalf
  • Start from the PTO Central home page and choose Submit PTO Request.
  • You normally submit for yourself. Authorized HR/Admin users can also submit a request on behalf of another employee when needed — the request is always recorded under the employee taking the PTO.
  • Select the PTO type (PTO, MHD, Sick, Bereavement, Parental Leave, Other Leave) and add a reason/note if helpful.
  • Choose your start and end dates (the end date is your last day off, inclusive).
  • Provide a backup contact — the person covering for you — and confirm coverage with them before you submit.
  • Your request is automatically routed to your manager for approval. When your manager is unavailable, HR can route a request to an alternate approver.
  • Please submit at least 7 days in advance whenever possible. Requests with less notice are flagged as short-notice for HR awareness — they still go through, but more notice means smoother coverage.
You don't need to submit PTO for MBP recognized holidays.

C. Sick Leave

Everyone
  • Sick requests are auto-approved by the system — no manager decision is needed.
  • If you're able to access the form, submit the Sick request yourself.
  • If you can't access the form, your manager or HR can submit it on your behalf.

D. Manager Approval

ManagersHR / Admin
  • When an employee submits a request, the manager receives it for review.
  • The manager (or the assigned alternate approver, or HR/Admin) can Approve or Reject the request, optionally adding a comment.
  • Approved requests automatically create calendar visibility (see the next section).
  • Rejected requests do not create any calendar events.
  • If time off is still needed after a rejection, the employee should submit a new request (for example, with adjusted dates) rather than reusing the rejected one.

E. Calendar Visibility

Everyone
  • Approved and Auto-Approved requests are automatically reflected on the appropriate calendars.
  • Two calendars are used for PTO visibility: your own calendar (an out-of-office event delivered as an invitation) and the myBasePay Corporate Calendar (company-wide visibility).
  • After your request is approved, check your calendar to verify the PTO event appears as expected — and accept the invitation so it shows as Out of Office.

F. My Requests

Everyone

Open My Requests to view your full PTO history and the current status of every request you've made. Statuses mean:

PendingWaiting for your manager (or assigned approver) to decide. ApprovedApproved — calendar visibility is created automatically. Auto-ApprovedApproved automatically by the system (for example, Sick requests). RejectedNot approved. Submit a new request if time off is still needed. CancelledCancelled after submission — no longer active.

Requests whose key starts with PTO-LEGACY- are imported historical records from the previous PTO system — see the PTO-LEGACY section below.

G. Cancel a PTO

EveryoneHR / Admin
  • Start from the PTO Central home page and choose Cancel a PTO.
  • Cancellations are processed through the PTO Central / HR workflow — the request's status changes to Cancelled and it stops counting as active PTO.
  • For approved PTO whose calendar events were created by PTO Central, cancellation removes those calendar events automatically.
Legacy note: some upcoming PTO was imported from the previous system (PTO-LEGACY-…). Those calendar events were created by the old system, so cancelling one of these records may require manual calendar cleanup — HR/Tech Support will handle removing the old event.

H. HR Center

HR / Admin only
  • The HR Center is restricted to HR/Admin users — other accounts cannot load its data.
  • View all PTO requests across the company in one dashboard.
  • Search and filter by employee, status, manager, date range, short-notice, and on-behalf requests.
  • Review statuses and full request details, including the audit history.
  • Cancel records when appropriate — the cancellation is recorded in the audit log, and PTO Central-created calendar events are removed automatically.
  • Export reports (CSV) of the currently filtered requests for HR reporting.

I. PTO-LEGACY Records

EveryoneHR / Admin
  • Records with keys like PTO-LEGACY-123 are historical PTO records imported from the legacy system.
  • They appear in HR Center and in each employee's My Requests, so your PTO history is complete in one place.
  • They preserve history — they should not be edited unless there is a specific, approved correction.
  • Some upcoming legacy records have calendar events that were created by the old system. PTO Central does not manage those events, so cancelling one of these records may require manual calendar cleanup by HR/Tech Support.
